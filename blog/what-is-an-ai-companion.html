What Is an AI Companion?

Learn what AI companion apps are, how they work, and what realistic expectations users should have before signing up.

Definition

An AI companion is a conversational app designed to simulate ongoing dialogue, emotional presence, or role-based interaction. Unlike basic chatbots, companion apps often include memory, personalization, and relationship-oriented prompts.

How they work

Most products use large language models with additional personalization layers. As you chat, the app adapts tone and suggestions based on your preferences and past sessions.

When they are useful

Many users rely on AI companions for light conversation, mood check-ins, or confidence-building social practice. They are not a replacement for licensed mental health care.
