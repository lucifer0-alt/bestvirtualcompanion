Are AI Companions Safe?

A practical safety guide covering privacy, emotional boundaries, and healthy usage habits for AI companion apps.

Privacy and data considerations

Always assume chat logs may be stored for service improvement. Review privacy policies and avoid sharing financial or medical identifiers.

Emotional safety

AI companions can feel highly personal. Set boundaries around session length and avoid overreliance when real-world support is needed.

Practical checklist

  • Use strong unique passwords.
  • Review account deletion and data export options.
  • Turn off notifications if usage becomes compulsive.
